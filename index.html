SELECTED WORKS

Skincare Launch Teaser

Tools: Photoshop & Canva

A monochromatic pink concept designed to build anticipation. I used a reflective glass effect at the bottom to ground the product and soft lighting to create a "glow" that matches the brand identity. The typography mixes a casual script font ("Stay Tuned") with bold, structural text to guide the viewer's eye.

Luxury Fragrance Reveal

Tools: Photoshop (Masking & Layers)

Designed to stop the scroll using the Depth Effect. By layering the bold "NEW" typography behind the bottle neck, I created a 3D sense of space. The dark, moody lighting and bottom reflection add a premium, mysterious feel suitable for a high-end launch.

High-Energy Menu Promo

Tools: Photoshop & Illustrator

A dynamic Diagonal Split layout designed to break the standard grid and create visual movement. I chose a Deep Red & Warm Beige palette specifically to trigger appetite psychology. The typography is angled to match the split, maintaining high energy while keeping the food as the focal point.

Sweet Cravings Campaign

Tools: Photoshop & Illustrator

A high-contrast promotional layout designed for retail impact. I used a split-color background to organize the visual hierarchy, placing the product at the center overlap to create depth. The typography mixes bold, structural headers with curved elements to mirror the round geometry of the doughnuts.

Automotive Legacy Tribute

Tools: Photoshop (Masking & Compositing)

A high-octane poster celebrating the 1969 Mustang. I used a Text Interlocking technique, placing the vehicle partially in front of the bold "1969" typography to create immediate depth. The stark black background ensures the "Candy Apple Red" paint remains the undisputed focal point.

Luxury Jewelry Promo

Tools: Premiere Pro & After Effects

A cinematic showcase designed for "The Jewel Cove." I utilized a Dark Mode aesthetic to ensure the diamond detailing pops against the background. The circular composition creates a "halo effect" around the pendant, while the elegant serif typography reinforces the "Crafting Luxury" brand identity.

Legal Services Portfolio

Tools: Figma & Wix Studio

A minimalist, high-trust interface designed for a legal professional. I utilized a Muted Taupe palette to convey stability and calmness. The typography pairs a classic Serif font for authority with clean Sans-serif navigation, while the circular image mask softens the layout to make the personal brand feel approachable.

Meditation App Landing Page

Tools: Figma & Spline

A minimalist interface designed to induce calm. I used a Dark Mode aesthetic to reduce eye strain, paired with a high-fidelity macro shot of a dandelion to symbolize lightness and breath. The UI elements are kept rounded and soft (pill-shaped buttons) to avoid sharp edges and maintain the "Zen" atmosphere.

Travel Storytelling UI

Tools: Figma & Webflow

A cinematic layout designed for immersive narratives. I utilized an Asymmetric Split composition, using heavy negative space on the left to let the photography breathe. This creates a sense of freedom and openness, perfectly matching the "Bahamas" travel theme.
